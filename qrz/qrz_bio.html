Welcome to my QRZ Page!

Greetings fellow hams! I'm a passionate amateur radio operator from Poland, operating under the callsign SP3FCK. I got my license in May 2025 and since then I have been actively exploring the world of ham radio. I enjoy exploring various aspects of our wonderful hobby, from HF DXing to Parks on the Air (POTA) activations.

I'm learning and experimenting with antennas construction and design.

I enjoy every new QSO and the opportunity to meet people from all over the world.

Feel free to browse through my station details, check out my recent POTA activities, and explore my ham radio awards gallery below. 73 and hope to work you on the bands!
