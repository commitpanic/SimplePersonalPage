Welcome to my QRZ Page!

Greetings fellow hams! I'm a passionate amateur radio operator from Poland, operating under the callsign SP3FCK. I got my license in May 2025 and since then I have been actively exploring the world of ham radio. I enjoy many aspects of this wonderful hobby, from HF DXing to Parks on the Air (POTA) activations.

I'm constantly learning and experimenting with antenna construction and design, always looking to improve my station and expand my knowledge.

Professionally, I’m a software developer, and in my free time I enjoy creating tools and software that can be useful for the amateur radio community.

I truly enjoy every new QSO and the opportunity to meet people from all over the world.

 

Feel free to browse through my station details, check out my recent POTA activities, and explore my ham radio awards gallery below. 73 and hope to work you on the bands!
