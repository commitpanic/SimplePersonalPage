How To Publish To QRZ.com

Follow these steps after exporting your page from the builder.

  1. Open your exported qrz_bio.html in a plain text editor (for example VS Code or Notepad++).
  2. Copy everything between:
    <!-- QRZ.com Bio Page Content - Copy everything between these comments -->
    and the final closing comment at the end of the generated block.
  3. Log in to qrz.com and open your profile Edit page.
  4. Go to the section where your bio HTML is edited and paste the copied content.
  5. Save changes on QRZ, then refresh your public profile page to verify layout and links.
If QRZ strips or alters formatting, export again and paste only the generated content block, without any extra HTML from outside that block.
